
# Quantum Air: Cognitive Architecture Framework

[![DOI](https://zenodo.org/badge/DOI/10.5281/zenodo.18928323.svg)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18928323)
[![License: CC BY 4.0](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-CC%20BY%204.0-lightgrey.svg)](http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

**Quantum Air** is a next-generation cognitive architecture designed by **Umair Siddiquie**. It moves beyond traditional linear reasoning by categorizing intelligence into **nine distinct thinking styles**, visualized through a unique "cognitive circuit board" and radial diagrams.

This repository serves as both a **philosophical blueprint** for balanced AI design and a **practical manual** for documenting research in cognitive science, addressing the critical ethical risks of losing human cognitive agency to autonomous systems.

---

## 🌌 Core Concepts

### The Nine Thinking Styles
Quantum Air decomposes intelligence into nine modular styles (e.g., Analytical, Creative, Systems, Ethical) that operate in non-linear synergy.

### Visual Semantics
The framework employs a standardized color scheme to denote function and ethics:
*   🔵 **Deep Blue:** Core Cognitive Functions
*   🟢 **Teal:** Integrative & Adaptive Layers
*   🟠 **Orange:** Ethical Constraints & Agency Preservation

### Ethical Foundation
A primary focus of Quantum Air is mitigating the psychological implications of superintelligence, ensuring humans retain **cognitive agency** within decentralized and autonomous environments.
